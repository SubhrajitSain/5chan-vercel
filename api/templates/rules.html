5chan Rules

Please adhere to the following rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all users:

  • No illegal content.
  • No spam or self-promotion.
  • Respect other users.
  • No personal attacks or harassment.
  • Keep discussions civil and on-topic.
  • Use appropriate language.
  • Do not share personal information.
  • No malicious content or links.
  • No harassment or bullying.
  • No impersonation of other users.
  • Follow the rules and stay on topic for a board.
  • Do not post copyrighted material without permission.
  • Do not post personal information of others.
  • Do not post content that promotes violence or hate speech.
  • Do not post content that is sexually explicit.
  • Not abiding these rules will result in permanent account deletion.
